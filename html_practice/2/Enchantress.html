Enchantress

Enchantress is a chess piece in Dota Auto Chess.

Piece Ability

Nature's Attendants

A cloud of wisps heals Enchantress and any friendly units nearby. Lasts 11 seconds.

  • ABILITY: No Target
  • DISPELLABLE: Cannot be dispelled
  • HEAL PER SECOND: 25
  • RADIUS: 500
  • WISPS: 3/5/7
  • COOLDOWN: 15
